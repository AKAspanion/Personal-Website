Arduino Barrier

This small electronics project was done by me and my teammates for the annual Technical Fest in our college, when I was in 2nd year. This was the first time that I did any hardware related project. The basic goal of this project was to create an afordable sensing technique that can sense incoming train in metro stations and underground tunnels. We used infrared transmitter and receiver to calculate the presense of metro train. This had two implications, firstly closing and opening of metro barriers at metro stations, secondly turning on the light in tunnels iff train is approaching. We just created one such sensing unit for demonstartion purposes, but the idea was to create a network of these small affordable sensor througout the entire station. These sensors would communicate with each other and perform the job more accurately.
